Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ starts at 50 units for most product categories. For trial orders, we are flexible — contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Can I request a sample before bulk order?

Yes. We offer free product samples to serious buyers. You only pay for shipping. We encourage samples before bulk commitments.

What is the production lead time?

Standard lead time is 15–20 working days after design approval and advance payment. Rush orders can be discussed.

Can you work from our custom design?

Absolutely. Send us your artwork, logo, or design concept. We digitize it in-house and show you a preview before production.

Which countries do you ship to?

We ship worldwide — USA, UK, Europe, Middle East, and beyond. We assist with export documentation and packaging.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer) and Western Union. Payment terms: 30% advance, 70% before shipment. Negotiable for long-term partners.
