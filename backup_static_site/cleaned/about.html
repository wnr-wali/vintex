Who We Are

Our Story

From a local embroidery unit in Lahore to a trusted international supplier — our journey in every stitch.

Vintex Traders

Craftsmanship That
Speaks Globally


Vintex Traders is a Lahore-based embroidery manufacturing unit with years of hands-on experience in producing premium embroidered textiles for both local and international buyers.

What started as a vision to bring Pakistan's rich embroidery heritage to the global market has grown into a fully-equipped industrial operation — with over 50 computerized embroidery machines running in our modern production facility.

We serve importers, wholesalers, fashion designers, and brands across the USA, UK, Italy, France, Germany, UAE, and Saudi Arabia — delivering embroidery that meets the highest international standards at factory-direct prices.

Our philosophy is simple: every order, whether 50 pieces or 5,000, gets the same dedication to quality, precision, and communication.
