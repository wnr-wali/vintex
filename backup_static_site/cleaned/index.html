Crafted in Lahore,

Vintex Traders is a Lahore-based embroidery manufacturing unit with years of hands-on experience producing premium embroidered textiles for both local and international markets.

Our strength lies in combining traditional Pakistani craftsmanship with modern machine precision — delivering embroidery that is consistent, beautiful, and export-ready for buyers across the USA, Europe, and the Middle East.
